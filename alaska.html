1 Alaska Cancellation Policy

Alaska Airlines offers an effective loyalty program and has been classified for its precious passengers. With COVID-19, travel flexibility is more important than ever. If you are willing to cancel your tickets with Alaska, it is easy now with the Alaska Airlines Cancellation policy.

Here you will find the things you need to know before making an Alaska flight cancellation. Furthermore, remember that the cancellation of Alaska Airlines Booking can only be made by its official site or application.

1.1 Some Points You Need To Know About Alaska Airlines Cancellation Policy

Apart from these, there are even some Alaska Airlines Ticket Cancellation Policy rules that may apply to your booking on cancellation. Therefore, you need to keep these points in mind before you actually cancel your flight booking.

  • Firstly, a passenger may cancel his ticket before the flight’s scheduled departure.
  • If you are not eligible for a refund, you can still cancel your Alaska Airlines booking and get the value as flight credit. This value can be used for your future travel and will be equivalent to your initial fare.
  • Suppose you have purchased the flight ticket from a third party like a travel agent or any website.
  • In that case, you need to directly get in touch with them for canceling your flights and refund process.
  • Furthermore, the airline will refund your cash tickets within 20 business days. And if they are credit card purchases, you will get a refund within seven business days.
  • However, as per the cancellation policy of Alaska airlines, you may be prepared to see them in your account within 14 business days.
  • The airline will physically inspect refunds for paper tickets. Therefore, you may bring it from where you purchased or mail it to the official mailing address of Alaska Airlines.
  • Besides, a peace of mind fee waiver only applies to flight booking by Alaska Airlines, which you can identify by a 13-digit ticket number starting with 027 (Alaska Airlines code).
  • If your ticket is issued by another airline and does not start with 027, it will be subject to the other airline’s policy (with or without exemption).

1.2 Alaska Airlines 24 Hours Flight Cancellation Policy

  • Passengers can rescind their flight reservation within some specific time of purchasing tickets under the 24 hours cancellation policy of Alaska Air.
  • Once you successfully cancel your booking within the risk-free cancellation period, you are eligible to ask for a full refund.
  • However, do not forget that the airline will charge the cancellation fee if your flight cancellation exceeds the 24 hours frame.
  • If passengers buy non-refundable flight tickets and cancel their booking within 24 hours, they will obtain credits.
  • Consequently, the refunded amount will be credited as eCredit in your Alaska Airlines wallet.

1.3 Alaska Airlines Flight Cancellation Fee

Passengers who want to cancel their flight booking must pay a fee. Besides, if you cancel your flight within a day of departure, you do not need to pay any charge. Due to this, you can enjoy flight booking cancellation for free. In this case, Alaska will refund the full amount of your flight ticket.

Furthermore, just look at the below scenario of cancellation fee:-

  • The standard cancellation charges will be charged on some specified tickets and canceled within 24 hours of the travel date.
  • Moreover, the cancellation fee depends on the trips, fares, and cabin classes after the period.
  • The airline charges $ 125 for domestic flight ticket cancellation.
  • Besides, Alaska charges you from $ 200 to $ 500 if you cancel international flight tickets.

1.3.1 Some Innovative Methods To Cancel Alaska Airlines Flight Bookings

According to the Alaska Airlines cancellation policy, passengers have multiple options to cancel their flight booking. Moreover, they can use the official website to cancel their flight reservation. Along with this, they can dial customer service or do a live chat with customer executives.

1.3.2 Alaska Airlines Cancellation Through Official Website:-

  • First, open the official website.
  • Second, log in to your account and fill in the credentials in the given space.
  • Now, Select the “My Trip” option.
  • You will see a page with your booking details.
  • After that, select the booking you want to cancel.
  • Next, click on the “Confirm” option to process the cancellation.
  • You will get a confirmation email from the airline. Once your cancellation is confirmed.

1.3.3 Alaska Airlines Cancellation through Toll-free Number:

  • First, you need to dial Alaska Airlines’ toll-free number, 1-800-654-5669.
  • Then, you will connect to the customer service executive.
  • Now, you have to share your booking details with them.
  • Along with that, you have to tell the reason for the flight cancellation.
  • After that, the customer executive will start your cancellation process.
  • At last, you have to wait for a few days to get a confirmation email.

1.3.4 Alaska Airlines Cancellation Through a Live Chat:-

  • Firstly, visit the “Contact Us” page on the official website of Alaska Airlines.
  • Find the “Start chat” option, and start your conversation by entering your name and other details.
  • A chat box will get connected with you when you share your booking confirmation code in chat.
  • Now, give them all your booking details so that they will start your process.
  • Finally, wait until you receive your flight cancellation confirmation email.

1.3.5 How Alaska Cancellation Policy Applies On Different Fare?

Saver Fares
If you cancel your Saver Fares on Alaska Airlines, you can only get a full refund within the risk-free 24-hour window. However, after that, you can not cancel your Saver fare tickets.

Main Cabin Fares
For your main cabin fares, Alaska airlines cancelation policy states that you won’t have to pay any charges. Therefore, you can cancel for free.

First-Class Fares
Furthermore, if you have purchased a First-Class fare on Alaska Airlines, you can cancel them free of charge. Starting from May 1, 2021, the airline has waived the cancellation fee for First Class fares.

Award Tickets
Simultaneously, like the first-class fares, if you cancel an Award ticket on Alaska Airlines, the airline will not charge any fee from you. Therefore, you can simply cancel your tickets without worrying about anything.

1.5 Alaska Airlines Refund Policy

Once your flight is canceled, you need to claim a refund. For that, you should know the refund policy. Moreover, Alaska Airlines’ refund policy may differ for different situations.

  • Under Alaska Airlines’ 24 hours cancellation policy, the airline will provide a full refund.
  • Therefore, the refund will be made using the same payment mode you used during the flight reservation.
  • If you make your flight cancellation after 24 hours of booking, a fee will be deducted from the refund.
  • Moreover, if you purchased a non-refundable ticket, the amount is added to the future wallet.
  • Also, passengers can use this amount for future flight bookings.

1.6 How To get Request For Refund On Alaska Airlines?

To request a refund, passengers can use both online and offline. Similarly, you can choose any one method according to your preference. Consider the below steps to ask for a refund.

1.6.1 Steps for Claim a Refund Online:-

  • First, go to the official website of Alaska Airlines.
  • Second, initiate a login into your account.
  • Choose the “Manage Booking” option.
  • Now, enter your booking details for the canceled flight.
  • You will see the details of our canceled flight reservation.
  • Next, open the canceled ticket and click on the “Refund” option.
  • Lastly, follow the other process to complete your refund procedure.

1.6.2 Steps for Request a Refund Offline:-

  • First, open the official website of Alaska.
  • Next, go to the “Contact Us” section.
  • Get the customer service number and call on the number.
  • After that, you will connect to the customer executive.
  • Now, you have to provide all the booking details to them.
  • Then, they will start your refund process. You must wait a few days to get a confirmation email or call.

1.6.5 Can I Get a Refund on Alaska Award Flights?

Getting a refund on Alaska Airlines is not a difficult task. But if you have a flight booked with points, i.e., an Award flight, things can be a bit tricky. However, why worry when you have an Alaska Airlines booking. The airline automatically refunds your ticket value back into your miles/points account within 24 to 48 hours of the request.

However, just in case, if you do not see any credit balance added to your account after two days of cancellation, make a call. You need to get in touch with Alaska Airlines customer service at 1 (800) 252-7522 and ask about the Alaska Airlines refund details.
