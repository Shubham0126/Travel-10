  • Canceling all JetBlue tickets within 24 hours of the booking time when the flight is not departing within the next seven days makes you eligible for a full refund. Also, the refund amount credit directly goes to your base account.

  • Cancellation of non-refundable tickets incurs some extra charges, which you must pay to receive the ticket amount in the form of credit. Only for the 24-hour risk-free period, the ticket value remains intact after that; it is necessary to pay a fee.
